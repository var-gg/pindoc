## Corporate contributions

If You are contributing on behalf of an employer, have a representative
of the employer with authority to bind the entity reach out through
the standard pull request CLA bot flow; the bot will route to the
Corporate CLA variant, which differs from this one only in Section 4
(representation of employer authorization) and Section 1 (definition
of "You" to include the entity and its designated employees).

## How to sign

You do not need to submit a separate form. The CLA assistant bot will
post a signing link on your first pull request. Clicking "I agree" on
that link is the binding act; GitHub's audit trail serves as the
signed copy.

If the bot is not available for any reason, you may sign manually by
opening an issue titled "CLA signature: &lt;your name&gt;" and copying the
text: "I have read the Pindoc CLA and I hereby agree to its terms."
Signing manually is an exceptional path — the bot is authoritative.
